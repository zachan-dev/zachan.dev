Requirements Specification

Requirements can be linked to the tests and test instances that are designed to verify that the requirement has been met.  The CoverageEntity object links the requirement to the other entity.

Requirements can also be linked to other requirements in a Trace relationship to enable checking the impact of a change to one requirement on a related requirement.
