Defect Tracking

 

A Bug can be linked to another Bug, or to a Req, Run, TSTest, Test, TestSet, or a Step of a Run. Therefore, at least one of a Link object's SourceEntity or TargetEntity must be a Bug, and both of them can be a Bug.
